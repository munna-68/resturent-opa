Authentic Kacchi Biryani, Cooked the Traditional Way

Kacchi Ghar is a traditional Bangladeshi restaurant dedicated to preserving the authentic taste of Dhaka-style Kacchi Biryani.

We use premium basmati rice, tender marinated goat meat, saffron-infused layers, and time-honored dum cooking techniques passed down through generations.
